Payreq MYOB Usage

Payreq needs to access your MYOB account to obtain the following details:

  1. Your MYOB Business Names and Identifiers to allow you to select the MYOB Business for invoice delivery. Only the Business Identifier is stored n Payreq.
  2. Your profile Username. This is stored in Payreq and is visible to your registered Billers.

No other information form MYOB is accessed by Payreq, registered Billers or any other third party.

Payreq will use your MYOB connection to create a Supplier Contact if authorised by you and push biller invoices to your MYOB InTray.
